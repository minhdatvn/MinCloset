Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI suggestion work?

The app sends anonymized information about your closet items and local weather to a powerful AI (Google's Gemini) to generate a personalized and reasoned outfit suggestion.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your data, including photos, is stored locally on your device. If you choose to back up, your data is stored securely in your own private cloud space on Firebase, protected by Google's security standards.

Why do I need to log in for backup?

Logging in creates a secure link between you and your data on the cloud. This ensures that only you can access, back up, or restore your closet information.
